Welcome to QuaZar!
Are you an undergrad in desperate need of visualising concepts? Or a postgrad in need of data visualisation? You might have 20 years of experience,
or you might have never seen a physics formula before. It does not matter. Regardless of background knowledge, everyone can get something out of QuaZar.
For level 0 users we can recommend having a look at the adorable kittens playing with a newtons cradle. For all other users you simply start by inputting
known values in the menu on the left side. Once you have finished you run the simulation by choosing one of the buttons on the right side.
